Plain English version: we do not sell your personal information. We use it only to communicate with you, run projects, and maintain our business operations.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us, request a quote, or work with us on a project. This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, project details, billing details, and any files or content you send us.

We may also receive limited technical information automatically when you visit the site, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, and pages viewed. This type of information is commonly available through hosting logs and standard website operations.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information to reply to inquiries, prepare proposals, deliver requested services, send invoices, manage projects, improve communication, protect our site, and keep business records.

  • Respond to contact requests and quote inquiries.
  • Plan, design, build, and support websites or digital services.
  • Send updates about active projects, invoices, or support issues.
  • Maintain basic records for accounting, legal, and operational needs.

3. When we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary to run our business or complete your project.

  • With hosting providers, domain registrars, payment processors, CMS tools, or other service providers involved in your project.
  • With contractors or collaborators working under confidentiality obligations, if needed to deliver your project.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. How long we keep information

We keep inquiry and project information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including communication history, project delivery, support, accounting, and legal compliance. Different records may be kept for different periods depending on the type of project and the reason they are needed.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. No website, email, or digital storage method is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies, third-party tools, and external links

This website may use standard browser storage, hosting logs, or basic site functionality that helps pages load and operate correctly. If third-party tools such as analytics, forms, hosting services, or social platforms are used, those tools may process data under their own privacy policies.

Our website may link to external websites such as Instagram, WhatsApp, or payment and hosting services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

7. Your rights and choices

You can contact us to request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask for corrections, or request deletion where appropriate. You may also choose not to provide optional information, but that may limit our ability to respond or deliver services.

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this site.

8. Contact and policy updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with the latest effective date.

For privacy-related questions or requests, email craftedwebworks@gmail.com or use our contact page.
